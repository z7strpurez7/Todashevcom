The CaveArcher Project

Welcome to the CaveArcher Project page, where you'll find all the information about my exciting 2D platformer game, incluiding gameplay and photos. This project has been a labor of love and I've put my heart and soul into every aspect of it. CaveArcherProject is a one-of-a-kind 2D platformer game built entirely from scratch using C++. I've hand-crafted almost every aspect of the game, with no external game engines involved.

What sets this game apart from others?

What sets CaveArcherProject apart is its unique combat system. You'll be armed with a bow that you control with your mouse, making it your main weapon. This means you have to combine both movement and aim in order to succeed! I hope you enjoy learning more about CaveArcherProject and I can't wait for you to experience it for yourself.
